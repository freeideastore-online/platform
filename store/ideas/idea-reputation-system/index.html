Idea Reputation System

A reputation layer where people earn recognition by improving ideas before they ever build a product.

This is the independent FreeIdeaStore idea book for Idea Reputation System. Each chapter is a working document for improving, challenging, validating, or graduating the idea.

Book Chapters

Chapter 1 Snapshot FreeIdeaStore should make product judgment visible. People earn reputation for useful critiques, evidence, pivots, prototypes, and kill signals. The main product is not the raw ide Chapter 2 Brainstorming Log - App stores reveal builders after something ships. - Idea stores can reveal judgment earlier. - A contributor who repeatedly improves ideas is valuable to founders, investors, and Chapter 3 Problem And Customer Good early-stage contributors are hard to identify. Profiles usually show finished work, not the thinking that shaped it. This system creates a public trail of useful thinking. Chapter 4 Research Notes - Badges must reward quality, not volume. - AI-generated noise is a serious risk. - The system should make it easier to invite real builders into ProAppStore, ProGameStore, or ProI Chapter 5 Design Sketch Contribution types: - Risk found. - Customer sharpened. - Evidence added. - Pivot proposed. - Prototype started. - Kill reason accepted. - Pro graduation support. Chapter 6 Prototype Plan - Add contribution types to each idea. - Let reviewers mark a contribution as useful. - Show contributor badges on profiles. - Track which contributors helped ideas graduate. Chapter 7 Validation Plan The system works if outside people can inspect a profile and quickly understand what kind of thinker or builder the person is. Chapter 8 Open Questions - Who judges contribution quality? - How do we prevent spam and badge farming? - Should reputation decay, specialize, or transfer across stores? Chapter 9 Contribution Prompts - Define the first badge set. - Design anti-spam rules. - Propose a review workflow. - Sketch a contributor profile page.
