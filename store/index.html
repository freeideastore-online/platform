People are the product.

  • Critiques show strategic judgment.
  • Pivots show creativity under constraints.
  • Evidence links show research discipline.
  • Prototype starts show execution bias.
  • Kill signals show honesty and taste.

Pro graduation.

  • Strong ideas become curated ProIdeaStore dossiers.
  • Top contributors are attached to the opportunity history.
  • Builders, investors, and operators can see who improved the idea.
  • Ready concepts can flow into ProAppStore or ProGameStore builds.
