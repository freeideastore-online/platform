About

A public workspace for early ideas.

FreeIdeaStore is where rough ideas can be published cheaply, challenged publicly, and improved by real contributors. The homepage is the catalog; this page explains the portal.

What belongs here

  • Raw ideas that need pressure.
  • Small one-page concepts.
  • Research notes and source-backed evidence.
  • Reasons to support, trash, or pivot an idea.

What does not belong here

  • Fake contributor profiles.
  • Inflated metrics.
  • Financial, legal, or medical advice pretending to be personal guidance.
  • Long pitch decks before the idea has earned them.

Why contributors matter

  • Good critique shows judgment.
  • Evidence shows research discipline.
  • Useful pivots show product taste.
  • Honest kill signals save time.

How ideas graduate

  • A strong idea can become a ProIdeaStore dossier.
  • The contributor history should travel with it.
  • Serious dossiers can later become app, game, web, or agent builds.
