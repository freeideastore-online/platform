# Open Questions

- Is the buyer a parent organizer, a club, a school-adjacent community, or nobody?
- Will parents install another app for a casual park group?
- Are birthday reminders genuinely useful, or do they feel awkward?
- What exact consent rules are needed for child photos?
- Should birthdays store only day/month and avoid birth years?
- Can appreciation streaks stay warm rather than competitive?
- Which existing tool feels most wrong after a real trial?
- Would anyone pay 5 to 15 dollars per month for private family profiles, birthday reminders, photo consent, attendance streaks, and new-family onboarding?
