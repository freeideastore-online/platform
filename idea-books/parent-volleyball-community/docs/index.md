# Parent Volleyball Community

A lightweight local family-circle idea for a Saturday volleyball and playground ritual.

The original idea is not really a sports team app. It is a local community memory problem: parents meet weekly near a playground and volleyball area, children play nearby, new families drift in, and the group wants a warmer way to remember names, birthdays, photos, consent, and regular attendance.

The current judgment is conservative: do not build a custom app yet. Trial BAND or Spond for six to eight Saturdays, measure whether people actually use birthdays, albums, RSVP, and attendance appreciation, then build only around the gap existing tools fail to cover.
