# Problem And Customer

The first user is the informal organizer of a Saturday parent group. The second user is any parent who wants to remember who people are, see photos safely, know birthdays, and feel included without turning the group into a formal club.

The original group wants to:

- keep basic member information;
- share photos within the trusted circle;
- remember birthdays of parents and children;
- get reminders for birthdays and weekly meetups;
- recognize people who come regularly, such as three Saturdays in a row;
- keep the activity informal, friendly, and family-oriented.

The likely payer is not every parent. If there is a payer, it is the organizer of a group, club, school-adjacent community, or recurring local activity. Even then, willingness to pay is uncertain.

The product is only worth building if existing tools feel clearly wrong after a real trial.
