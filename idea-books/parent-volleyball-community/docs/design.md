# Design Sketch

Only design a custom app around the real gap:

- family profiles instead of individual-only profiles;
- parent and child birthdays in one safe place;
- child photo consent and private sharing rules;
- memory of who attended regularly;
- warm appreciation for repeated participation;
- lightweight onboarding for new families;
- a friendly local-circle feeling, not a formal sports team.

Possible custom scope:

- join by QR code or invite link;
- private circle approval;
- family profile with parent names, child names, and birthday month/day;
- optional child age range;
- photo albums by Saturday/date;
- photo consent setting per family;
- Saturday check-in;
- automatic streaks, such as three weeks in a row;
- birthday reminders;
- new-this-week introductions;
- export and delete controls.

Avoid at first:

- full chat;
- public discovery;
- payments;
- complex league features;
- large social feeds;
- public photo sharing.
