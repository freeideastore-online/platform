# Slowdown Personal Reset

A lightweight wellbeing app concept focused on slowing down, reflection, and daily reset behavior.

Slowdown already exists as a small FreeAppStore app at slowdown.freeappstore.online. The idea book should capture what has been built and what still needs validation: a local-first emotional reset app that recommends one short practice from a mood, energy, mental speed, and tension check-in.

The product boundary is important. It is a wellbeing tool only. It should avoid diagnostic, treatment, crisis-service, or medical claims.
