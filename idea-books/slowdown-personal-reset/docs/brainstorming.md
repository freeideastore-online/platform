# Brainstorming Log

- The product should not compete with large meditation platforms.
- The useful wedge is an immediate personal reset: pause, name the pressure, choose the next small action.
- Retention matters more than feature count.
- The app should feel useful in under one minute.
- The check-in should be concrete: mood, energy, mental speed, and tension.
- The result should be one practice, not a menu of 40 things.
- Saved resets may become valuable if they show gentle patterns without turning into surveillance.

The interesting idea is not "another meditation app". It is a short decision aid for the moment when someone feels sped up, tense, foggy, or overloaded.
