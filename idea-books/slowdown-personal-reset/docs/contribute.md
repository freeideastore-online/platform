# Contribution Prompts

- Test the app for seven days.
- Propose a sharper onboarding flow.
- Find comparable small wellbeing tools.
- Suggest retention metrics that do not reward unhealthy checking.
- Review the wording for accidental medical or therapy claims.
- Suggest safe short practices that fit mood, energy, mental speed, and tension states.
- Test offline/local-first behavior.
- Test optional Firebase sync without making sync feel required.
- Propose a simple export/delete data flow.
- Add evidence about when people actually need one-minute reset tools.
