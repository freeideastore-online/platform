# School Transport Options

A practical guide and product idea around safe school transport options for Australian children aged 5 to 10.

The researched finding is that Australia does not appear to have a universal on-demand "Uber for primary-school kids" available everywhere. The useful product may be a trusted comparison, verification, and coordination layer across school-run transport, child-focused rideshare, scheduled child taxis, and nanny or babysitter pickup services.

The current recommendation is practical: check school-managed transport first, then child-focused booked services, then supervised pickup options. Do not use standard Uber for unaccompanied children aged 5 to 10.
