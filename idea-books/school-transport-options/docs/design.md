# Design Sketch

Start with a trusted options page per school area:

- Public transport routes.
- Walking school bus groups.
- Licensed providers.
- Parent coordination requests.
- Safety checklist.
- School-specific notes.

The product should show a clear best-fit path:

1. School already has managed transport: use the school-run route or StudentRide-style provider.
2. No school route, but the child only needs transport: check Shebah or a child-focused booked service if active locally.
3. Sydney or Melbourne and scheduled transport needed: verify whether Kabs4Kids is active in the suburb.
4. Child needs adult supervision before or after the trip: use a nanny or babysitter pickup service such as Kiddo.
5. Standard Uber for an unaccompanied 5 to 10 year old: not suitable.

Each option page should include:

- minimum child age;
- adult handover requirement;
- driver checks;
- Working With Children Check status;
- child-seat handling;
- booking model;
- tracking and visibility;
- geographic coverage;
- direct source links;
- verify-before-use warnings when information may be stale.
