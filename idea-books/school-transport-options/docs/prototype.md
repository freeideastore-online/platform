# Prototype Plan

- Choose one Australian suburb.
- List school-managed routes and official school transport pages.
- List Shebah availability and booking constraints.
- Verify whether Kabs4Kids is active locally.
- List nanny/babysitter pickup options such as Kiddo.
- Explain why Uber for Teens does not apply to ages 5 to 10.
- Include child-seat and handover guidance.
- Show the date each option was checked.
- Interview 15 parents and 5 school or after-school administrators.

Do not prototype a live driver marketplace yet.

The first app can be a console for maintaining verified local pages:

- provider name;
- coverage area;
- age fit;
- booking link;
- verification evidence;
- safety notes;
- last checked date;
- parent comments or corrections.
