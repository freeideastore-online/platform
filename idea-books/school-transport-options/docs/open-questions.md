# Open Questions

- Can this work without becoming a regulated transport marketplace?
- Who verifies providers?
- What data can be public without creating safety issues?
- How often do provider availability and coverage change?
- How should stale provider listings be flagged?
- Can the product verify Working With Children Checks without storing sensitive data?
- Who is responsible if a parent acts on outdated information?
- Should this stay as a guide, or become a booking workflow only with vetted partners?
- Would parents pay, or would schools/providers sponsor local pages?
