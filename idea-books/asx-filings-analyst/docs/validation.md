# Validation Plan

Success is not "did it pick winners." Success is whether users return weekly, trust the citations, and find companies they would not have noticed otherwise.

Manual validation plan:

1. Create a weekly ASX watchlist for four weeks using public reports and basic financial data.
2. Share it with 10 to 20 Australian retail investors.
3. Ask what they distrust, what saved time, and whether they would pay.
4. Measure whether they come back for the next report.
5. Only build software after confirming that the weekly workflow is valuable.

Strong signals:

- users ask for the next report;
- users forward it to other investors;
- users say the source links saved research time;
- users discover companies they would not have checked;
- some users are willing to pay before the full app exists.

Weak signals:

- users only look at the ticker list;
- users ignore the source evidence;
- users ask for buy tips rather than research support;
- users cannot explain why they trust the output.
