# Problem And Customer

Retail investors do not have time to read every ASX announcement. They want a small set of companies worth investigating, with links back to filings and a clear explanation of uncertainty.

The first customer is a careful DIY investor who already reads reports but wants help narrowing the field.

The likely early user is not a day trader. It is someone who:

- follows ASX companies manually;
- wants a weekly research rhythm;
- prefers facts and source links over social media tips;
- understands that cheap can mean risky;
- wants to compare valuation, quality, growth, and risk in one place.

The pain is not "I need a magic answer." The pain is "I do not have time to read all filings, notice all changes, and remember why each company is on my watchlist."

The app should respect that the user makes the final judgment.
