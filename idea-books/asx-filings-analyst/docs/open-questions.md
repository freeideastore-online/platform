# Open Questions

- Which public market data can be used without licensing problems?
- Should this be a consumer product, a research newsletter, or a tool for investment clubs?
- How should disclaimers, suitability, and advice boundaries be handled?
- Which ASX universe is narrow enough for an MVP: ASX 100, ASX 200, ASX 300, or selected sectors?
- Should pricing data be user-uploaded, licensed, delayed, or omitted from the first test?
- How will the app prevent source extraction errors from becoming confident false claims?
- Which claims require exact citation before being shown?
- Should the first business model be subscription, paid report, investment club tool, or internal research workflow?
- What review process is needed before a generated watchlist is published?
